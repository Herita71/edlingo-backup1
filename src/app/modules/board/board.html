Downtime

As much as we may love our jobs, the simple truth is that most of us live for el fin de semana, or the weekend.

Being able to talk about weekend plans, then, is critical in Spanish.

Phrases that use the verb ir are common when people talk about where they're going.

¿Qué vas a hacer este fin de semana?

What are you doing this weekend?

Voy a la playa.

What are you doing this weekend?
